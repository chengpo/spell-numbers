Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Spell Numbers! Here I describe the privacy policy of Spell Numbers.

What information be collected?

Spell Numbers does not collect any user information.

Why does Spell Numbers request Camera permission?

Spell Numbers needs to use Camera for Text Recognition. Spell Numbers does not store or send any picture taken by Camera.

How do I revoke Camera permission?

User may revoke Camera permission at anytime :
  1. On your device, open the main Settings app.
  2. Tap Apps or Application Manager depending on your device, this may look different).
  3. Tap "Spell Numbers" App.
  4. Tap Permissions.
  5. Next to a permission you want to turn on, move the switch to the right until it turns green. If you want to turn a permission off, move the switch to the left until it turns gray.
